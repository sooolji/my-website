First Year Computer Science Journey 🚀🎓

As a first-year computer science student at Universidad de Oriente in Santiago de Cuba, Cuba, I've had the opportunity to explore a wide range of fascinating topics! 🇨🇺💻 Let me share with you what I've learned so far in my academic journey. 🌟

First Semester 📚

In my first week, I was introduced to the exciting world of computer science and then dove into the following courses:

Programación I (Programming I) 🐱‍💻

  • Algorithms and computers
  • C++ Introduction
  • Control structures
  • Subprograms
  • Arrays
  • Pointers and dynamic variables
  • Character strings
  • Files

Álgebra I (Algebra I) 🧮

  • Complex numbers
  • Advanced polynomial topics
  • Linear and matrix equations
  • Vector spaces and linear applications

Análisis Matemático I (Mathematical Analysis I) 📊

  • Numerical sequences
  • Limits and continuity of functions
  • Derivatives and differentials
  • Integral calculus and its applications

Lógica (Logic) 🧠

  • Sets, relations, and functions
  • Propositional logic
  • Normal forms
  • Logic circuits
  • Predicate calculus
  • Propositional deduction
  • Mathematical induction

I also had the opportunity to join a Chess Club as part of my Educación Física (Physical Education) course! 🏅🧠 Additionally, I explored the history of computing in Historia de la Computación (History of Computing) and delved into philosophical topics in Filosofía (Philosophy). 🕰️📚

One of the most fun assignments was writing "una entrevista con chatgpt" (an interview with ChatGPT), where I had the chance to interview an AI! 🤖💬

Second Semester 📚

In the second semester, I continued my journey with the following courses:

Educación Física (Physical Education) 🏃‍♂️🏅

I remained active in the Chess Club, honing my strategic thinking skills.

Práctica Laboral e Investigación I (Professional Practice and Research I) 🔬🖥️

I had the opportunity to provide a graphical user interface (GUI) for a C++ project, putting my programming skills to the test.

Historia de Cuba (History of Cuba) 🇨🇺📜

As the name suggests, I learned about the fascinating history of my home country.

Curso Optativo (Elective Course) 💡

In this course, we explored how the internet works and discussed the differences between office suites like LibreOffice and Microsoft Office. 💻📊

Programación II (Programming II) 🐱‍💻

  • Introduction to classes and objects
  • Operator overloading
  • Generic functions and classes
  • Inheritance and polymorphism
  • Recursion
  • Linear lists
  • Standard Template Library (STL)
  • Error handling
  • Graphical interface and Qt

Álgebra II (Algebra II) 🧮

  • Analytical geometry
  • Quadratic forms
  • Euclidean spaces
  • Algebraic structures

Análisis Matemático II (Mathematical Analysis II) 📊

  • Series
  • Limits and continuity of functions of several variables
  • Derivatives and differentials of functions of several variables
  • Integral calculus and its applications for functions of several variables

As you can see, my first year has been packed with exciting topics and challenges! 🎉 I'm eager to continue learning and growing as a computer science student. 🌱🚀
